Glowing Happiness

Glowing Happiness is a partially a guide, part modlist, to achieve how I think Fallout 4 should've been.
As per usual, this is yet another take to have an enhanced vanilla-like setup, without too many alterations.

Features and goals

  • Close to Vanilla
  • Lore friendly
  • Modularity

Requirements

  • Fallout 4 (PC version) with all DLCs.
  • The High Resolution Texture Pack DLC is not installed.
  • A computer that can handle Fallout 4 on atleast Low settings.
  • A new/clean game installation, preferably outside of windows UAC protected folders.
